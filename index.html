Welcome to Your Home in Koh Lanta

Escape the crowds and discover a true local gem at Phongphiphat Lanta Mansion. We pride ourselves on creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere where you can genuinely relax. Our property is nestled in a calm, quiet neighborhood, surrounded by lush tropical gardens that offer a peaceful retreat after a day of exploring. Located just a short 500-meter stroll from the beautiful Klong Toab beach, we offer the perfect blend of comfort and tranquility. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, our goal is to provide the warm, personal hospitality that makes a holiday in Koh Lanta truly unforgettable.

Our Location

Find Us Here

Our prime location offers the best of both worlds: serene tranquility and easy access to local attractions. We are just a 5-minute walk from the soft sands of Klong Toab beach, a perfect spot for swimming and watching the sunset. Within a short stroll, you'll also discover a variety of authentic local restaurants and charming cafes. We are a perfect base for exploring the island, with the famous Lanta Old Town just a scenic 15-minute scooter ride away.
